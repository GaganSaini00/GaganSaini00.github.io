Sb Weather 

My Final project at Humber

The Sb Weather Station is a minimal variant of the high-level weather stations and controlled by Sb Weather Android application.  The app was designed to be easy to use and readable for anyone who is interested
in monitoring weather on their own. We hope to bring new people into the field of meteorology
and raise awareness around the issues of climate change.

All the measurements are live and coming from the sensors, but first they are uploaded to firebase and then pulled to the Sb Weather app.
